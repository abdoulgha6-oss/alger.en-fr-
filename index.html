La Complainte des mendiants
arabes de la casbah

The Lament of the Arab Beggars
of the Casbah

Written in Paris in October 1951, published by Jean-Paul Sartre in Les Temps Modernes, this poem is a long cry of pain born from a news story — a nine-year-old beggar girl, Yasmina, killed by her father in the streets of Algiers. A foundational text of Algerian literature, long suppressed, now restored.
